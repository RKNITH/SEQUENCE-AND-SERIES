Arithmetic Series

An arithmetic series is the sum of the terms of an arithmetic sequence.

The nth term of an arithmetic series:

a_n = a + (n - 1)d

The sum of an arithmetic series with n terms:

S_n = n/2( 2a + (n-1)d)
